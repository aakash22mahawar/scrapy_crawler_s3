Expanding through Strategic Real Estate Development with Steve Venezia, Planet Fitness Franchisee
April 23, 2024
Taj Adhav
Leasecake
is lease and location management made easy for the franchise industry, providing a pain-killing application that helps proactively oversee one of the largest risks in their business – their real estate leases.
In their webinar, Steve Venezia, VP of Leasing and In-House Counsel at Core Development & Management, an independent franchisee of Planet Fitness, delves into the intricacies of expanding a retail franchise.
