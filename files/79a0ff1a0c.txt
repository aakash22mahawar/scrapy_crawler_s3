As the most experienced and multi-skilled industry advisory firm, they assist in optimizing the financial performance of clients’ businesses, position clients to maximize the value of their businesses, while guiding their clients and managing the complex transactions to help achieve clients’ goals. C Squared's long-term clients include multi-unit franchisees, franchisors, independent brands, family businesses, large corporations, investors, lenders, private equity investors and family offices.

With his help, we were able to secure the necessary funds to finance our next phase of growth. I couldn't be more grateful for their partnership and look forward to our continued success together." - Laddi Singh, Popeye's multi-unit franchisee.</blockquote>
