Orchatect, headquartered in Richardson, TX, is a dynamic company in the SaaS space that is gaining market traction across various channels via their groundbreaking Business Management system. Orchatect’s flagship product, Maestro® is an easy to use business intelligence and optimization platform built for business owners, managers, and employees. Orchatect’s proprietary and unique solution orchestrates various business systems, applications, and platforms via single pane of glass. This allows the business owners to focus on the details and address issues as our platform alerts you to them. With this, we save you time and money and enable you to work ON your business, not IN it.
