Founded in 1991, Fishman PR is the franchise industry’s leading public relations and content marketing agency. Our strategic, results-driven franchise lead generation and brand awareness campaigns have helped hundreds of franchisors attract quality franchisees and customers.
Using your brand’s franchisee and customer personas as a basis, our specialized integrated process first generates earned media coverage that carries the right messages to the right media outlets at the right times to your prospects. Our content and digital marketing strategies then drive prospects back to your website, where they find compelling multimedia content designed to convert leads into sales.
