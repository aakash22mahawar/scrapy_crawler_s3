<a href="https://franchisesuppliernetwork.com/featured-supplier/devhub/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">DevHub</a> is a CMS truly built for franchises - where you can scale and integrate freely with the support your need, when you need it. DevHub is in the business of helping franchise brands, and they put together a handy 2024 guide to digital franchise marketing.
<p dir="ltr">In this <a href="https://www.devhub.com/stat/cache/files/guide-to-digital-franchise-marketing_1.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Guide to Digital Franchise Marketing</a>, you’ll learn how to:</p>

<ul>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Build an impactful digital activation strategy for each franchisee</p>
</li>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Make the most of Google Map Pack and Google Business Profiles</p>
</li>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Build (and maintain) your national and local SEO</p>
</li>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Capitalize on positive customer reviews</p>
</li>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Use direct-to-consumer channels to drive loyalty</p>
</li>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Equip new franchisees with competitive local marketing skills and tools</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p dir="ltr">Plus, insights on:</p>

<ul>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Managing your marketing in-house vs. through an agency</p>
</li>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">The benefits of diversifying your marketing suppliers</p>
</li>
 	<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">The actions, channels, and enhancements to increase revenue, multi-unit ownership, and customer loyalty.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p dir="ltr">View the full guide <a href="https://www.devhub.com/stat/cache/files/guide-to-digital-franchise-marketing_1.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">here</a>.</p>
