In our ongoing Supplier Discovery Day series, we tap experts in our network to discuss buzzy topics in the industry. This time, we're focusing on employee recruitment - a HOT topic right now in franchising (and nearly every other industry).

Jake Fishman dug into the topic with 3 suppliers that assist in finding the perfect management team for your franchise brand:
<ul>
 	<li>Ross Weisman from <a href="https://franchisesuppliernetwork.com/featured-supplier/epic-managers/">Epic Managers</a></li>
 	<li>Neil Young from <a href="https://franchisesuppliernetwork.com/featured-supplier/two-ladders/">Two Ladders</a></li>
 	<li>Anthony Andre from <a href="https://franchisesuppliernetwork.com/featured-supplier/pandologic/">PandoLogic</a><b></b></li>
</ul>
Here are some of their key takeaways:
<ul>
 	<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">Creating a <strong>strong, human hiring processes will allow you to find the best fit managers for your franchise</strong>. Utilizing a company with hiring experts can help franchisors <span style="font-weight: 400;">cross the co-employment barrier to make a positive change in leadership.</span></li>
 	<li aria-level="1"><span style="font-weight: 400;">There is a high necessity for delivering an intuitive user experience for job seekers. <strong>How they perceive a company's application process directly shapes their view of the workplace itself.</strong></span></li>
 	<li>Analyzing ghosted/rejected applications and understanding more about those individuals can help you<strong> tailor your future messages</strong> and direct budget into areas that will help you find excellent talent in niche areas that will best benefit your franchise system.</li>
</ul>
<strong><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dva2J2zlh88" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Watch here for more Employee Recruitment tips</a></strong> and stay tuned for our next discovery day!
