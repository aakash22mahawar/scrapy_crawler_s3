Franchise Supplier Wire 009: How To Dominate Google Maps
April 24, 2023
Shaun Salehyan
Ryan Hicks
·
Supplier Wire 009: How To Dominate Google Maps, with Shaun Salehyan - CEO of Map Ranks
You have an amazing business. Maybe you’re struggling to find that target audience that you know would benefit from connecting with you. But whatever you try, you just can’t reach them. This ultimately has to do with the SEO of your franchise and services.
In this episode, we break down a very important topic: Google Maps. We dive deep into a set of best practices as it relates to what you can do to get your business more organic visibility on the world’s number 1 search engine.
