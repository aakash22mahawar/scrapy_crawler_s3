Supplier Wire 036: How to Scale Your Franchise with Lizzy Young of DevHub
DevHub
is a CMS truly built for franchises – where you can scale and integrate freely with the support you need, when you need it.
In this Supplier Wire Series episode of Modrn Business Podcast, FSN VP of Business Development Jake Fishman chats with Lizzy Young, Director of Franchise Engagement for DevHub. Jake & Lizzy discuss Lizzy’s years of experience in franchising, and how DevHub’s world-class CMS helps Zors and Zees scale & grow.
