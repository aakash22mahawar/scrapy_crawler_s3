Supplier Wire 014:  Matthew Gourgeot, Head of Franchising at Thryv
Thryv
is a small business software platform that provides a variety of tools to help businesses manage their operations. The platform offers features such as online appointment scheduling, customer relationship management (CRM), social media management, online presence management, reputation management, payment processing, and more. Thryv is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to small business owners who may not have a lot of experience with technology. It is intended to help these businesses streamline their operations, attract new customers, and grow their revenue!
In this episode of the Franchise Supplier Wire series on Modrn Business Podcast,
Matthew Gourgeot, Head of Franchising at Thryv
, talks all things management and marketing for emerging home service franchise organizations.
