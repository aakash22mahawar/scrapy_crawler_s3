For over 30 years, MFV Expositions has been producing the leading franchise events worldwide that consistently offer high-quality, face-to-face opportunities for prospective franchisees to meet franchisors looking to grow their brand. MFV hosts franchise expos in the U.S., Mexico, Germany and the U.K, highlighting brands from every industry & every investment level, and offers a robust conference program for all to attend.
