<h2>Become a part of OUR extended franchise family.</h2>
To most, breaking into the franchise industry can be daunting. To our FSN Team, the franchise community is like big, happy family. After many years of hard work, our team wanted to leverage the influence from our relationships, our events and our podcast to do one thing: help other suppliers succeed in an industry we love. With that, our annual Supplier Membership Program was born!

If you’re struggling to figure out why being a part of our “Franchise Family” is a must, here are some of the values we bring to our supplier members:
<ul>
 	<li>Industry brand awareness</li>
 	<li>Authority within the franchise space</li>
 	<li>In-depth understanding of your company’s “ideal customer profile”</li>
 	<li>Collaboration with other FSN members</li>
 	<li>Deeper connections with the franchise community</li>
 	<li>Consultation on “go-to market strategy”</li>
 	<li>Increase of lead flow within the franchise industry</li>
 	<li>Higher deal velocity</li>
</ul>
<h4>In addition, the FSN Membership brings a multitude of perks for you and your brand:</h4>
<ul>
 	<li>FSN Website Listing</li>
 	<li>FSN Membership Badge</li>
 	<li>FSN Member Portal For:
<ul>
 	<li><em>Content Submission</em></li>
 	<li><em>Deal Announcements</em></li>
 	<li><em>Profile Optimization</em></li>
 	<li><em>FSN Supplier Training Program</em></li>
 	<li><em>Industry-Categorized Franchisor Database</em></li>
</ul>
</li>
 	<li>FSN Member Virtual Meet N’ Greets</li>
 	<li>Franchise Supplier Wire Podcast Episode</li>
 	<li>90-Second “Value Prop” Video</li>
 	<li>Newsletter &amp; Social Media Distribution Of FSN Member Content</li>
 	<li><em>Coming Soon: FSN Member Online Community, In-Person Supplier Summit, Supplier Disclosure Document (SDD)</em></li>
</ul>
If you and your franchise could benefit from these perks, join <a href="https://franchisesuppliernetwork.com/supplier-memberships/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">HERE</a>.
