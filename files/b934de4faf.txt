PandoLogic is a wholly owned subsidiary of Veritone, Inc. (NASDAQ: VERI), a leader in enterprise artificial intelligence. PandoLogic is a global market leader in programmatic recruitment and generative conversational AI solutions that effectively source, qualify and engage job candidates through intelligent automation. PandoLogic’s customers consistently report significant increases in recruiter productivity, better candidate quality, faster hiring time, and reduced talent acquisition costs. More than a thousand top employers rely on PandoLogic to power their unique, specific recruiting requirements by driving superior, predictable outcomes. To learn more, please visit www.pandologic.com
