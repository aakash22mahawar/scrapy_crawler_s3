Supplier Wire 007: How Franchisors Can Leverage Storytelling, Franchise Filming CEO Trevor Rappleye
Sharing a story about your business is a powerful tool. When done right, you can make people feel connected to your franchise and get in front of potential franchise candidates.
In this episode of Franchise Supplier Wire,
FranchiseFilming
CEO Trevor Rappleye tells how his business helps accomplish this. Specifically, he speaks on how this can be beneficial for franchises. Aligning with a clear strategy can improve business, culture, and more.
