<p class="p1">For over two decades, <a href="https://franchisesuppliernetwork.com/featured-supplier/scorpion/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span class="s1">Scorpion</span></a> has helped local businesses leverage technology and marketing to reach their goals. Scorpion helps businesses understand their local market and optimize their marketing efforts to drive more revenue. Through award-winning technology and dedicated people, Scorpion helps businesses run their best business—unifying every part of their business towards reaching their goals.</p>
<p class="p1">To kick off our recent Supplier Discovery Day: Marketing Agencies series, we talked to Gabriella Ferrara, Vice President of Strategic Sales, Franchise at Scorpion about what makes them unique in the industry.</p>
<p class="p1">A few of our key takeaways:</p>

<ol class="ol1">
 	<li class="li1"><b>Find your Most Valuable Customer: </b><i>Utilize A.I. in digital marketing to really understand your leads and find the best customers for your franchise.</i></li>
 	<li class="li1"><b>Not All Leads are Created Equal: </b><i>Scorpion uses A.I. help you target leads who are </i><b><i>actually</i></b><i> leads with an abundance of valuable data for your sales and marketing teams.</i></li>
 	<li class="li1"><b>Ensure Clean Data: </b><i>Scorpion allows franchise brands to look at their revenue data and what’s actually driving their ROI.</i></li>
 	<li class="li1"><b>Ranking A.I.: </b><i>Prioritize the strategies to do at the local level when it comes to your SEO, based on the needs per location and market.</i></li>
 	<li class="li1"><b>Leads A.I.: </b><i>Train your A.I. to actually understand what it means to have intent of a lead so that a lead score can be provided and a value can be attached to each lead.</i></li>
 	<li class="li1"><b>Increased Transparency: </b><i>Use data from A.I. to find where shifts need to be made in the approach to closing leads, whether it’s on the sales or marketing sides.</i></li>
</ol>
