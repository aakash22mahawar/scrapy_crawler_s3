Supplier Wire 022: Improve Your Customer Experience with Delightree
Delightree
is a cutting-edge AI-powered training & operations app that revolutionizes the way you manage your business. It accelerates employee onboarding and training, transforming your standard operating procedures into an interactive digital format that encourages queries and fosters understanding. Delightree centralizes communication, creating a unified platform for owners and employees to interact and collaborate. With automated forms and checklists, it simplifies inspections and audits, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks. Delightree is a tool your entire team will love and actually use. With our promise of getting you up and running in days, not weeks, and our commitment to providing best-in-class support, Delightree is the ultimate solution for modern, efficient franchise operations.
In this episode of the Franchise Supplier Wire series on Modrn Business Podcast, FSN CSO Dean Hatzitheodosiou sits down with
Tushar Mishra, Co-Founder of Delightree,
to discuss his role in helping franchisees improve their customer experience by aggregating customer reviews and providing location-wise insights.
