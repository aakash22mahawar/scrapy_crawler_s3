CareerPlug is a software company on a mission to solve the #1 challenge of running a business: attracting and hiring the right people. CareerPlug works with over 360 franchise brands to help make hiring easier for their franchisee’s, so that they can more successfully run and grow their businesses. Career was built on a proven hiring playbook that helps franchise businesses attract, evaluate, and hire the right applicants. With our easy to use software, you can create an attractive job posting in seconds, automatically post jobs to the major job boards, and easily evaluate and compare candidates so that you end up with the right people in your roles.
