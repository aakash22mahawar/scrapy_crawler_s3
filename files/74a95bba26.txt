Headquartered in Schaumburg, IL, Paylocity (NASDAQ: PCTY) is an award-winning provider of cloud-based HR and payroll software solutions. Founded in 1997 and publicly traded since 2014, Paylocity offers an intuitive, easy-to-use singular platform that is easy to deploy, streamline HR and payroll processes with ease.  An easy, singular site enables your franchisees to better attract and retain employees.  We uniquely serve the franchise industry by providing real-time data on labor costs, employee sentiment, market-pay data and other valuable insights so that franchisee owners can make data-driven decisions.   Most importantly, we offer hundreds of full API interface options for other 3rd party software so that ALL of your information is accurate.  Get back to running your business and let us help you easily understand your number related to human capital.
