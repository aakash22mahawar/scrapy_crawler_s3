FSW 006 - How Insurance Compliance Can Improve Your Exit Strategy
Ensuring that your franchise is connected with the right resources is our specialty – especially when it involves help you didn’t know you needed. Rikor is a company that verifies insurance compliance. This is a resource that is so important to optimize success and business.
Episode 6 of Franchise Supplier Wire features Rikor’s CEO Wade Millward. We deep dive into the topic of insurance compliance and explore how Rikor helps franchise systems make sure coverage does not lapse and extra economies of scale are captured.
