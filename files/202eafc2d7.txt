Supplier Wire 028: Real Estate & Technology with Jonathan Hill of Morrow Hill
Morrow Hill
is the premier firm for the largest global organizations to both exclusively represent tenant lease negotiations and offer our clients a single point of contact.
In this Franchise Supplier Wire Series episode of Modrn Business Podcast, Dean Hatzitheodosiou, the FSN Chief Sales Officer, chats with Jonathan Hill, Co-Founder of Morrow Hill. Morrow Hill’s unique client team model ensures that every transaction is managed with care and precision, with no outsourcing to outside parties.
Jonathan & Dean chat through all things real estate & technology in franchising, and what franchise brands should expect in 2024.
