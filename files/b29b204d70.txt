<a href="https://franchisesuppliernetwork.com/featured-supplier/morrow-hill/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Morrow Hill</a> is the premier firm for the largest global organizations to both exclusively represent tenant lease negotiations and offer our clients a single point of contact.

In this Franchise Supplier Wire Series episode of Modrn Business Podcast, Dean Hatzitheodosiou, the FSN Chief Sales Officer, chats with Jonathan Hill, Co-Founder of Morrow Hill. Morrow Hill's unique client team model ensures that every transaction is managed with care and precision, with no outsourcing to outside parties.

Jonathan &amp; Dean chat through all things real estate &amp; technology in franchising, and what franchise brands should expect in 2024.
