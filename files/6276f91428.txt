Biller Genie is an award-winning, cloud-based accounts receivable platform that improves cash flow by seamlessly integrating popular accounting software (like QuickBooks) with all major payment providers and automates bill presentment, overdue invoice follow up and late messages, online payments, and back office reconciliation. Each franchisee receives their own branded portal where clients can log in to pay invoices, view invoice history, store payment methods, and set up auto pay. The entire experience is branded with your Franchise’s colors and logos, making it easy for your customers to manage their accounts online and strengthening brand loyalty. Franchisees using Biller Genie typically realize a 40% reduction in overdue invoices and a reduction in DSO by 14 to 15 days. By automating repetitive tasks, Biller Genie frees up labor resources so franchisees can focus on growth, not administrative busywork. Improve cash flow and put A/R on autopilot with Biller Genie!
