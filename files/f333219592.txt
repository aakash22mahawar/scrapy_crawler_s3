Supplier Wire 035: Driving Franchisee Reviews & Revenue with Oscar Wimshurst of NiceJob
NiceJob
is a Reputation Marketing platform helping franchisees get 4x the reviews and 2x the sales. It is the all-in-one reputation marketing software for busy and budget-conscious business owners.
In this Supplier Wire Series episode of Modrn Business Podcast, FSN VP of Business Development Jake Fishman chats with Oscar Wimshurst, Senior Partnerships Manager of NiceJob. Jake & Oscar discuss Oscar’s background, what “reputation marketing” is, and how NiceJob drives franchisee revenue.
