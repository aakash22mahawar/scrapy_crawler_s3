Franchisors partner with Rikor to protect their brand from reputational harm and unintentional exposure. Rikor offers a done for you insurance compliance solution through proprietary software that takes the burden off the plate of the operations team. Franchisors also partner with Rikor to improve the franchisee insurance purchasing experience. Through Rikor a franchisee can purchase the coverage they need to not only meet FDD requirements but to also protect their investment. Rikor has created a frictionless experience for the franchisee to get exactly what they need with minimal effort.
