<a href="https://franchisesuppliernetwork.com/featured-supplier/c-squared-advisors-llc/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">C Squared Advisors</a> is an independent investment bank that provides financial and transaction advisory services to multi-unit owners in the restaurant and franchise industries. They were formed to provide our clients with relevant industry expertise from experienced, practical and qualified professionals. <strong>They also understand the unique needs and challenges franchisees and franchisors face during a transaction.</strong>
<h4>C Squared Advisors offers an array of services including:</h4>
<ul>
 	<li>Mergers and Acquisitions
<ul>
 	<li>Full or partial sale of a company</li>
 	<li>Acquisition of a company</li>
 	<li>Merger between companies</li>
</ul>
</li>
 	<li>Financing
<ul>
 	<li>Debt and/or equity raise for acquisitions, new unit development and remodels</li>
 	<li>Total refinancing and debt consolidation</li>
</ul>
</li>
 	<li>Refranchising
<ul>
 	<li>Divestiture of company-owned units</li>
 	<li>Market and organization planning</li>
 	<li>Identification and qualification of prospective buyers</li>
</ul>
</li>
 	<li>Valuation
<ul>
 	<li>Exit strategy planning</li>
 	<li>Partner buy/sell agreements</li>
 	<li>Dispute resolution</li>
 	<li>Estate planning</li>
</ul>
</li>
 	<li>Workouts/Restructures
<ul>
 	<li>Partnership Dissolution</li>
 	<li>Workouts/lender issues</li>
 	<li>Turnaround situations</li>
</ul>
</li>
 	<li>Advisory Services
<ul>
 	<li>Lender covenant compliance reporting</li>
 	<li>P&amp;L optimization</li>
 	<li>Project analysis and implementation</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
