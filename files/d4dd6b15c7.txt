Commercial Investigations LLC (CI) is your go-to destination for comprehensive background investigations in the world of franchising! As a Woman Owned, licensed private investigator, we pride ourselves on being the one-stop shop for all your needs.  Partnering with CI means embracing a commitment to quality and unmatched customer service.  With over two decades of expertise, our team is prepared to deliver services that cater to your unique needs, ensuring a seamless experience.  Our expertise extends across four key areas, ensuring a thorough exploration of potential franchisees, their businesses, the employees working at franchisee locations, and even the franchisor’s corporate team.
