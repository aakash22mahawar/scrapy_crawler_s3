SeoSamba’s Franchise Marketing Operation System, or FMOS, is an integrated marketing and sales automation toolset that lets you combine and impact all digital key performance indicators (KPI’s) through social, email, reputation, SEO, paid from the same dashboard.
SeoSamba’s Franchise Marketing Operating System combines all tools required to power a modern marketing and sales department and saves its users time and money over point software solutions by grouping a review management platform, email marketing platform, social marketing platform, web chat-to-text mobile app, lead tracking software, and search engine optimization automation platform, under a single roof. SeoSamba’s FMOS framework also includes a powerful CRM solution built for multi-location businesses in mind and a specific CRM edition built for franchise development.
